About us

SHAPE YOUR LIFE HEALTH & WELLNESS offers a unique service where our ultimate goal is to enhance the quality of human life through physical training and passive treatments, by providing a service to everyone. Whether at work, home or gym in your area. We offer employee wellness programs, a fun filled healthy environment at work by offering classes on company premises as well as Aroma Therapy, Ashiatshu, Indian Head Massage and other treatments. We also offer full body assessments done at your home with up to 60 home exercise routines to suit your needs. We endeavour to provide not just healthier lifestyles at work or home but also to communities that they can shape by introducing health and wellness to the public. Our future plan is to open up a chain of health club where everyone can benefit and prosper.
